Description: Polychromatic earthenware vessel with two spouts and a twisted handle connecting the spouts. The body has a white ground with black curvilinear and geometric designs with orange fill. The spouts have an aroange graound with black designs with white fill. The handle is orange. The artist signed the bottom of the vessel. It reads, “L. Kiyite Zuni. NM 87327, Box #1226, 2/22/90.”
